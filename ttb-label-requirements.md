# TTB Mandatory Label Requirements by Alcohol Type

> Source: U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB)  
> Regulations: 27 CFR Parts 4 (Wine), 5 (Distilled Spirits), 7 (Malt Beverages) + 27 CFR Part 16 (Health Warning)

---

## 🍺 Beer / Malt Beverages
**Governing regulation:** 27 CFR Part 7  
**Note:** All mandatory items may appear on any label on the container. There is no "same field of vision" requirement.

### Always Required

| Item | Details | CFR Citation |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Brand name** | Must not mislead on age, origin, or identity | 27 CFR 7.64 |
| **Class and type designation** | e.g. "Beer," "Ale," "Lager," "Stout," "Malt Beverage" | 27 CFR 7.141 |
| **Net contents** | Volume in container; may be blown, embossed, or molded into container | 27 CFR 7.70 |
| **Name and address** | Bottler/importer name + city/state; may be blown or embossed | 27 CFR 7.66–7.68 |
| **Health warning statement** | Exact Surgeon General wording; "GOVERNMENT WARNING" in bold caps | 27 CFR Part 16 |

### Conditional Requirements

| Item | Trigger | CFR Citation |
|------|---------|--------------|
| **Alcohol content (ABV)** | Required only if alcohol is derived from added flavors or non-beverage ingredients (other than hops extract) | 27 CFR 7.63(a)(3) |
| **FD&C Yellow No. 5 disclosure** | If colorant is used | 27 CFR 7.63(b) |
| **Cochineal extract or carmine disclosure** | If either colorant is used | 27 CFR 7.63(b) |
| **Sulfite declaration** | e.g. "Contains Sulfites" — if product has ≥10 ppm sulfur dioxide | 27 CFR 7.63(b) |
| **Aspartame disclosure** | If used as a sweetener | 27 CFR 7.63(b) |
| **Country of origin** | Imported products only; per CBP rules | 27 CFR 7.69 |

### Additional Notes
- All mandatory info must appear in **English** (brand name is the only exception)
- **Caps, corks, foil capsules, and the bottom of the container** cannot be used to satisfy mandatory information requirements
- Puerto Rico: labels may appear solely in Spanish

## 🍷 Wine
**Governing regulation:** 27 CFR Part 4  
**Note:** Applies to wines with **≥7% ABV**. Wine below 7% ABV is regulated by FDA food labeling rules instead, plus the health warning statement.

The label is split into two zones: the **brand label** carries some items; others may appear on any label.

### Must Appear on the Brand Label

| Item | Details | CFR Citation |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Brand name** | Must not mislead on origin, age, or identity | 27 CFR 4.33 |
| **Class and type designation** | e.g. "Red Wine," "Chardonnay," "Sparkling Wine," "Table Wine" | 27 CFR 4.32 |
| **Appellation of origin** *(conditional)* | Required when a varietal, vintage date, or estate claim is made, or when required by class/type | 27 CFR 4.23, 4.27, 4.32 |
| **Percentage of foreign wine** *(conditional)* | Required if the label references foreign wine in a domestic-foreign blend | 27 CFR 4.32 |

### May Appear on Any Label

| Item | Details | CFR Citation |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Alcohol content (ABV)** | "Table wine" or "light wine" may substitute for wines 7–14% ABV; otherwise stated as a percentage | 27 CFR 4.36 |
| **Net contents** | Stated in metric units (e.g. 750 mL); may be embossed on the bottle | 27 CFR 4.37 |
| **Name and address of bottler/importer** | Name + city/state per basic permit; preceded by "Bottled by" or "Packed by" | 27 CFR 4.35 |
| **Sulfite declaration** | "Contains Sulfites" required if ≥10 ppm SO₂; exemption requires TTB-certified lab analysis | 27 CFR 4.32(e) |
| **Health warning statement** | Exact Surgeon General wording; "GOVERNMENT WARNING" in bold caps | 27 CFR Part 16 |
| **Country of origin** *(conditional)* | Imported wine only; per CBP requirements | 27 CFR 4.34(e) |
| **FD&C Yellow No. 5 disclosure** *(conditional)* | If colorant is used | 27 CFR 4.32(c) |
| **Color ingredient disclosure** *(conditional)* | If certain artificial coloring is added | 27 CFR 4.32 |

### Additional Notes
- Minimum type size: **1 mm** for containers ≤187 mL; **2 mm** for larger containers
- Allergen declarations (milk, egg, fish, wheat, etc.) are **voluntary** but must be complete if any allergen is listed
- If a varietal name is used, ≥75% of the wine must be made from that grape variety (or higher per state law)
- Multiple varietals in a blend: percentage of each grape must be listed, totaling 100%

## 🥃 Distilled Spirits
**Governing regulation:** 27 CFR Part 5

### ⚠️ Same Field of Vision Requirement
The following three items **must all be visible simultaneously** on one side of the container without turning it. For cylindrical containers, one "side" = 40% of the circumference.

| Item | Details | CFR Citation |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Brand name** | Name under which the product is sold; must not mislead on age, origin, or identity | 27 CFR 5.64 |
| **Class and type designation** | e.g. "Vodka," "Bourbon Whisky," "Blended Scotch Whisky," "Chocolate Flavored Brandy"; or a distinctive/fanciful name plus statement of composition | 27 CFR 5.141, 5.165 |
| **Alcohol content (ABV)** | Stated as "Alcohol __% by volume"; proof may also appear but must be visually distinguished from the ABV statement | 27 CFR 5.65 |

### May Appear on Any Label

| Item | Details | CFR Citation |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Net contents** | In metric units (e.g. 750 mL); must meet an approved standard of fill | 27 CFR 5.70, 5.203 |
| **Name and address of bottler/importer** | Name + city/state per basic permit; immediately preceded by "Bottled by," "Imported by," etc. | 27 CFR 5.66–5.68 |
| **Health warning statement** | Exact Surgeon General wording; "GOVERNMENT WARNING" in bold caps | 27 CFR Part 16 |
| **Country of origin** *(conditional)* | Imported spirits only; per CBP requirements | 27 CFR 5.69 |
| **Sulfite declaration** *(conditional)* | "Contains Sulfites" if product has ≥10 ppm SO₂ | 27 CFR 5.63(c)(7) |
| **Coloring material disclosure** *(conditional)* | e.g. "Colored with Caramel" or "Artificially Colored" | 27 CFR 5.63(c)(6) |
| **FD&C Yellow No. 5 disclosure** *(conditional)* | Specific statement required if used | 27 CFR 5.63(c)(5) |
| **Cochineal extract or carmine disclosure** *(conditional)* | If either is used | 27 CFR 5.63(c)(6) |
| **Commodity statement — neutral spirits** *(conditional)* | For blended/rectified spirits using neutral spirits: must state the % of neutral spirits and the commodity (grain, etc.) from which distilled | 27 CFR 5.71 |
| **Treatment with wood** *(conditional)* | For whisky/brandy treated with wood other than oak containers: "Colored and flavored with wood ___" | 27 CFR 5.73 |
| **Statement of age** *(conditional)* | Mandatory for whisky aged <4 years and grape brandy aged <2 years; also required when any age representation is made | 27 CFR 5.74 |
| **State of distillation** *(conditional)* | For certain US whiskies, required if not distilled in the state shown in the address | 27 CFR 5.66(f) |

### Additional Notes
- Minimum type size: **1 mm** for containers ≤200 mL
- A **COLA (Certificate of Label Approval)** is required before bottling for interstate commerce; apply via TTB's COLAs Online system
- Spirits sold only in the state of bottling may instead obtain a **Certificate of Exemption** and must include "For sale in [State] only"

## Health Warning Statement (All Three Categories)

Required on every alcohol beverage with ≥0.5% ABV sold or distributed in the US. Exact wording per 27 CFR Part 16:

> **GOVERNMENT WARNING:** (1) According to the Surgeon General, women should not drink alcoholic beverages during pregnancy because of the risk of birth defects. (2) Consumption of alcoholic beverages impairs your ability to drive a car or operate machinery, and may cause health problems.

**Formatting requirements:**
- "GOVERNMENT WARNING" must be in **bold capital letters**
- Statement must appear as one block, separate from other label information
- Minimum type size applies (varies by container size)

# TTB Alcohol Labeling Stipulations

## Distilled Spirits

### Required on a Label

| Field | Requirement | Placement | Conditional Logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Brand Name | Required | Label | Always required |
| Class / Type Designation | Required | Label | Always required |
| Alcohol Content | Required | Label | Always required |

### Required on Any Label

| Field | Requirement | Placement | Conditional Logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Health Warning Statement | Required | Any label | Required for alcohol beverages at 0.5% ABV or more |
| Name and Address | Required | Any label | Always required |
| Net Contents | Required | Any label | Always required |

### Conditionally Required

| Field | Requirement | Placement | Conditional Logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Age Statement | Conditional | Any label | Required for certain distilled spirits depending on class/type, age, and standards of identity |
| Color Ingredient Disclosure | Conditional | Any label | Required if the product contains FD&C Yellow No. 5, cochineal extract, or carmine |
| Commodity Statement | Conditional | Any label | Required for certain neutral-spirit, redistilled, compounded, or blended distilled spirits products |
| Country of Origin | Conditional | Any label | Required for imported products |

## Wine

### Required on Brand Label

| Field | Requirement | Placement | Conditional Logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Brand Name | Required | Brand label | Always required |
| Class / Type Designation | Required | Brand label | Always required |

### Required on Any Label

| Field | Requirement | Placement | Conditional Logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Alcohol Content | Required | Any label | Always required |
| Health Warning Statement | Required | Any label | Required for alcohol beverages at 0.5% ABV or more |
| Name and Address | Required | Any label | Always required |
| Net Contents | Required | Any label | Always required |

### Conditionally Required on Brand Label

| Field | Requirement | Placement | Conditional Logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Appellation of Origin | Conditional | Brand label | Required when the label includes a vintage date, varietal designation, type designation of varietal significance, semi-generic designation, estate bottled claim, or certain product names qualified with “brand” |
| Percentage of Foreign Wine | Conditional | Brand label | Required if foreign wine is present and disclosure is applicable |

### Conditionally Required on Any Label

| Field | Requirement | Placement | Conditional Logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Color Ingredient Disclosure | Conditional | Any label | Required if the wine contains FD&C Yellow No. 5, cochineal extract, or carmine |
| Country of Origin | Conditional | Any label | Required for imported wine |
| Sulfite Declaration | Conditional | Any label | Required if total sulfur dioxide is 10 ppm or more |

## Beer / Malt Beverage

### Required Mandatory Label Information

| Field | Requirement | Placement | Conditional Logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Brand Name | Required | Mandatory label information | Always required |
| Net Contents | Required | Mandatory label information | Always required |
| Class / Type Designation | Required | Mandatory label information | Always required |
| Name and Address | Required | Mandatory label information | Always required |

### Required on Any Label

| Field | Requirement | Placement | Conditional Logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Health Warning Statement | Required | Any label | Required for alcohol beverages at 0.5% ABV or more |

### Conditionally Required on Any Label

| Field | Requirement | Placement | Conditional Logic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Color Additive Disclosure | Conditional | Any label | Required if applicable color additives are present |
| Sulfite Declaration | Conditional | Any label | Required if total sulfur dioxide is 10 ppm or more |
| Aspartame Declaration | Conditional | Any label | Required if aspartame is present |
| Alcohol Content | Conditional | Any label | Required if alcohol comes from added flavors or other added nonbeverage ingredients containing alcohol, except hops extract; otherwise optional unless state law requires or prohibits it |
| Country of Origin | Conditional | Any label | Required for imported malt beverages |
